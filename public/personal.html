Core Values

Authenticity

I believe in showing up as who I truly am — unfiltered, imperfect, and real. Authenticity is the foundation of every meaningful relationship.

Curiosity

The world is too fascinating to stop asking "why". Curiosity isn't just a trait — it's the engine of my growth.

Integrity

I say what I mean and mean what I say. In a world full of noise, being consistent and trustworthy matters deeply to me.

Growth

Every day is a chance to be slightly better than yesterday. I embrace discomfort as a signal that I'm expanding.

Purpose

I don't want to just do things — I want to do meaningful things. Purpose gives direction to all the effort.

Excellence

Not perfectionism — but the genuine desire to put out work that I'm proud of and that helps others.
